Creating Contact information for the footer:

Create a block called contact information and place the block into the footer region of the block administration area. Then click configure and put your contact information in as follows filling in the general info with your actual details:

<p>Office Location • 1 Harpst St., Arcata, CA 95521 • 707.826.xxxx • <a href="#">Contact Us</a>.</p>
